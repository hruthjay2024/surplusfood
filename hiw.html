How It Works

We connect restaurants, events, and food donors with volunteers and NGOs to rescue surplus food and deliver it to people in need.

Our 4-Step Process

🍽️

1. Surplus Food Identified

Restaurants, hotels, or events notify us when they have extra food that is still fresh and safe to eat.

📞

2. Pickup Request Placed

Donors submit a pickup request through our website. Our team immediately alerts nearby volunteers.

🚗

3. Volunteers Collect Food

Verified volunteers collect the surplus food following safety and hygiene standards.

❤️

4. Food Distributed

The collected food is delivered to shelters, orphanages, and needy communities to reduce hunger.
